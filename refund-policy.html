Revision & Refund Policy

Effective date: July 14, 2026 · Last updated: July 14, 2026

VidaSong creates customized artistic products based on information and creative preferences supplied by each customer. Because personalized songs cannot ordinarily be resold to another customer, revisions, cancellations, and refunds are handled according to the following policy.

1. Song-Plan Approval

Before payment and production, VidaSong will ordinarily provide a written song plan summarizing matters such as:

  • The intended recipient
  • The occasion
  • Important story details
  • The requested language
  • The intended musical genre or direction
  • The primary emotional message

The customer is responsible for reviewing the plan and correcting inaccurate names, dates, relationships, pronunciations, facts, or creative instructions before approving it.

Production begins only after:

  1. The customer approves the song plan;
  2. VidaSong confirms the order; and
  3. Required payment is received.

Submitting the initial request form does not require the customer to purchase a song.

2. Included Revisions

The number of included revisions is determined by the package or written quote selected by the customer.

A revision means one consolidated request submitted after receiving a draft or initial delivery.

Included revisions may cover reasonable changes such as:

  • Correcting a factual error made by VidaSong
  • Correcting a name pronunciation when the customer previously supplied accurate pronunciation guidance
  • Correcting a spelling error
  • Replacing or adjusting a limited number of lyric lines
  • Removing an unintended reference
  • Making a moderate adjustment to wording, balance, or emphasis
  • Correcting a technical defect in a delivered file

Customers should include all requested changes in one message whenever possible. Separate messages concerning the same delivery may be treated as one revision round.

3. Changes That Are Not Standard Revisions

The following requests may require an additional fee, a major-remake fee, or a new order:

  • Changing the recipient
  • Changing the occasion
  • Changing the language after production begins
  • Changing to a substantially different genre
  • Changing the requested vocal direction after production
  • Replacing most or all of the approved story
  • Adding substantial new information that was not included in the approved plan
  • Requesting a complete lyric rewrite after approving the original direction
  • Reversing creative preferences that the customer previously approved
  • Requesting additional versions beyond the purchased package
  • Correcting an error that originated in inaccurate information supplied by the customer

VidaSong will explain any additional charge before beginning work outside the purchased revision scope.

4. Revision Deadline

Revision requests must ordinarily be submitted within seven calendar days after the relevant delivery.

If no revision request is received during that period, the order may be treated as accepted and complete.

VidaSong may consider later requests when practical, but late revisions may require an additional fee and availability is not guaranteed.

5. Revision Turnaround

Most standard revisions are completed within approximately two to three business days.

This is an estimate rather than a guarantee. Complicated revisions, customer delays, technical issues, or unusually high order volume may require additional time.

6. Cancellations Before Production

A customer may cancel for a full refund if:

  • Payment has been made; and
  • VidaSong has not begun lyric development, music generation, artwork production, or other customized production work.

The customer should request cancellation as soon as possible by emailing orders@vidasong.com.

7. Cancellations After Production Begins

Once customized production has begun, payments are generally nonrefundable because time, creative work, and production resources have already been committed to an order that cannot be resold.

A change of mind, changed occasion, recipient disagreement, or preference for a different creative direction does not ordinarily qualify for a refund after production begins.

The available remedy will normally be the revisions included with the purchased package.

8. When a Refund Will Be Provided

VidaSong will provide an appropriate full or partial refund when:

  • VidaSong is unable to produce or deliver the purchased service;
  • A duplicate payment was processed;
  • VidaSong materially fails to follow the approved song plan and cannot reasonably correct the problem through the included revisions; or
  • A refund is otherwise required by applicable law.

Any amount refunded will depend on the portion of the service that was not properly provided.

9. Matters That Do Not Ordinarily Qualify for a Refund

Refunds are not ordinarily provided solely because:

  • The recipient did not react as expected
  • The customer no longer needs the song
  • The relevant event was canceled or rescheduled
  • The customer supplied incorrect facts or pronunciation information
  • The customer changed their preferred genre, language, vocalist, or story after approval
  • The customer prefers a different artistic interpretation even though the delivered song substantially follows the approved plan
  • A social-media platform, music service, or third party refuses or limits use of the song
  • The customer failed to download or preserve delivered files

Personalized music is subjective. VidaSong does not guarantee a particular emotional response, musical preference, or reaction from the recipient.

10. Delivery Delays

Quoted delivery dates are good-faith estimates unless VidaSong expressly agrees in writing to a guaranteed deadline.

A customer-caused delay, including delayed approval, incomplete information, or delayed revision feedback, will extend the expected delivery date.

If VidaSong anticipates a substantial delay, it will communicate with the customer and provide a revised estimate.

If VidaSong cannot deliver the order within a reasonable period and the customer does not agree to an extension, the customer may request a refund for the undelivered service.

11. Rush Orders

A rush order is accepted only when VidaSong confirms the deadline in writing and the applicable rush fee has been paid.

Paying or requesting expedited service without written confirmation does not create a guaranteed delivery deadline.

12. Downloading and Preserving Files

Customers are responsible for downloading and safely storing delivered song, artwork, lyric, and video files.

VidaSong may attempt to redeliver recently completed files, but permanent file storage and indefinite redelivery are not guaranteed.

13. Chargebacks and Payment Disputes

Customers should contact VidaSong at orders@vidasong.com before initiating a payment dispute so that VidaSong has an opportunity to investigate and resolve the issue.

Nothing in this section limits rights that cannot legally be waived.

14. Contact

Revision, cancellation, and refund requests should be sent to:

orders@vidasong.com

Please include the customer's name, order email address, and a description of the requested resolution.
