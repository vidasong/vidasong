FAQ

Questions families ask us

Is this made with AI?

Our songs are created through a combination of human storytelling, hand-written and edited lyrics, and AI-assisted music production. Every order is personally reviewed and refined by a real person before delivery — names, pronunciation, cultural details, everything.

Can the song be in Spanish, or both languages?

Yes. We offer songs fully in English, fully in Spanish, or bilingual — for example, Spanish verses with an English chorus, perfect when generations in one family speak different languages. You describe the sound you want in your own words, and we match it.

How does payment work?

You place your request first — no payment is taken on this site. Within one business day we send you a written song plan. Only when you approve the plan do we send a secure payment link, and production begins after payment. If we can't do your story justice, we'll tell you and nothing is owed.

What if I don't like the song?

Every package includes revisions, and because you approve the song plan before production, surprises are rare. If a detail is wrong, we fix it.

Is my family's story kept private?

Yes. Your stories, photos, and songs are private by default. We never use a customer's song or story in our marketing without separate written permission from you.

Can it sound like a specific famous artist?

You can absolutely reference artists, eras, or songs to describe the sound you want — that's exactly what the request form is for. We create an original song inspired by that style, but we never imitate or clone a real artist's actual voice.
